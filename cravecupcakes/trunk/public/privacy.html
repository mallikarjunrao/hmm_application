PRIVACY/CANCELLATIONS AND REFUND POLICY


CRAVE CUPCAKES®, recognizes and respects the importance of maintaining our customers' privacy. All information collected from you on our website is considered confidential and is used for the purpose of conducting general business practices. We will never sell or disclose any information to any third party.

All credit card transactions on this website are processed the day you place the order. In order to secure your order you must use a credit card. Changes or cancellations to an order placed online must be made with the store by calling 713.622.7283 at least 24 hours prior to your order's pickup or delivery date.
