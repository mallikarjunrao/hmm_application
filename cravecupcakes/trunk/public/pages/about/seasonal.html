HAPPY HOLIDAYS

The Holidays are here and we are celebrating with two Holiday traditions, Eggnog and Gingerbread. Our EGGNOG cupcakes are topped with nutmeg butter cream frosting and sugar holly berries. Our HOLIDAY GINGERBREAD cupcakes are topped with cream cheese frosting and sugar gingerbread men.
Both Holiday flavors will be available everyday from December 1st thru 24th. We will close at 5pm on December 24th and will be closed Christmas Day.
