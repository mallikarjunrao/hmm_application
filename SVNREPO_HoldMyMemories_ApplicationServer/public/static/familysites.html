Family Websites
Where do you hold your family’s memories? Are they photos in a box, videotapes on a shelf, your children’s artwork in a drawer? We designed HoldMyMemories.com to be the place to hold, organize, and share your family’s memories.

There are many social networking sites out there; Facebook, MySpace, and LinkedIn just to name a few. These are great sites for high school students, college kids, and business associates wanting to connect, but may not be the best way for families to share memories. You also have many choices when it comes to storing your photos and videos online. Some of these sites are great for storage, but may not be the best for organizing and sharing.

At HoldMyMemories.com we don’t compete with the other social networking sites, we integrate with them. Your photos, videos, and other memories can be easily shared with your Facebook, MySpace, Twitter, and other accounts. You can also easily send your memories to your friends via email.

A HoldMyMemories.com site is organized by "chapters." You can organize your chapters any way you would like. Each family member can have a chapter, you can have a chapter for vacations, holidays, or other events. Inside each chapter are subchapters. Your daughter, for example, could have a chapter with her name on it. Her subchapters could be "Birthdays," "Artwork," "Recitals," "School," or any important category of memories.

One of the best features of HoldMyMemories.com is our network of HoldMyMemories.com licensed studios. If you visit a HoldMyMemories.com Studio, that studio will post your images to your account, allowing you to share those special photo sessions with family and friends, post your images to Facebook, and share in ways that were previously not possible with images taken at a professional studio! Features and benefits vary by HoldMyMemories.com studio location. Search for a studio near you by entering your zip code in the search box on this page.
