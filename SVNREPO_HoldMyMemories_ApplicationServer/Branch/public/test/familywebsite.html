Family Websites

Do you have a family? Where do you keep your family memories? Are they photos in a box, videotapes on a shelf, your children’s artwork in a drawer?

We designed HoldMyMemories.com to be the place to hold, organize, and share your family memories.

There are many social networking sites out there; MySpace, FaceBook, and Linkedin just to name a few. These are great sites for high school students, college kids, and business associates to connect, but may not be the best way for families to share memories. You also have many choices when it comes to storing your photos and videos online. Some of these sites are great storage mediums (and a good way to order photos), but may not be the best for organizing and sharing. We created HoldMyMemories.com to be a place for families to hold, organize, and share those important family memories.

A HoldMyMemories.com site is organized by "chapters". Each family member can have a chapter. There can be a chapter for family vacations and family holidays. Inside each chapter are subchapters. Your daughter, for example, could have a chapter with her name on it. Her subchapters could be “Birthdays”, “Artwork”, “Recitals”, “School”, or basically any important category of memories.
